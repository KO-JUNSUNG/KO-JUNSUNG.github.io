My background is in statistics and machine learning, and these days I
apply that toolkit to autonomous UAV systems — estimation, control, and
multi-drone coordination. This site is where I organize what I'm
studying along the way.

What you'll find here
======
This is a **TIL (Today I Learned)** and study log — notes I write to
organize what I'm learning and revisit later. Right now I'm working
through **swarm control** and related topics:

- **Multi-agent systems** — swarm control, formation control, cooperative behavior
- **Estimation & navigation** — Kalman filtering, sensor fusion, GPS-denied navigation
- **Learning-based control** — reinforcement learning, Sim2Real
- **Foundations** — the statistics and ML underneath all of the above

Background
======
I come from statistics and hold an M.S. in Artificial Intelligence. I'm
not a roboticist by training — I'm someone who uses statistical and
learning-based methods to tackle problems in autonomous systems, and
drones happen to be where those problems get interesting for me.
